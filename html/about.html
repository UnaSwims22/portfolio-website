About Me

I'm an interaction designer and developer passionate about creating systems that shape behaviour, guide interaction, and create meaningful digital experiences.

Who I am

I'm a designer and developer who believes that interaction design is fundamentally about systems. Rather than focusing on isolated visual outputs, I investigate how systems guide behaviour, how mechanics influence decision-making, and how feedback loops shape user experience.

My background spans interaction design, game systems, UX analysis, and technical implementation. This combination allows me to bridge the gap between design intent and technical execution, creating systems that work as designed.

My Approach

I approach every project as a systems investigation. This means:

  • Systems Thinking: Understanding how components interact and influence each other
  • UX Analysis: Grounding design in user needs and interaction affordances
  • Technical Competency: Combining design thinking with implementation expertise
  • Reflective Practice: Continuously analyzing, iterating and improving
